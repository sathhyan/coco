COCO PEAT

Coco peat is made by processing the coconut husk and soaking in low salt content water.Which has two types high ec and low ec in this high ec isn't soaked in water.

COCO FIBER

Fiber is another product from coconut husk it will be processed 2 times in the machine and the clear fiber is made into bundles and coir ropes and many fiber products.

HUSK CHIPS

Husk chips is made from coconut husk in which the husk is cut in to small pieces and long fibers are removed in this product also low ec and high ec is available.
